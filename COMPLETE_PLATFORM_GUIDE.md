## üìö CONTENT PHILOSOPHY

### Storytelling Approach

Every lesson follows a narrative structure:

1. **Hook**: Grab attention with a question or problem
2. **Context**: Why this matters (real-world impact)
3. **Explanation**: Break down complex concepts simply
4. **Examples**: Real trade scenarios with visuals
5. **Application**: How to use it immediately
6. **Pitfalls**: Common mistakes to avoid
7. **Practice**: Interactive exercises

### Progressive Disclosure

- Start with high-level concepts
- Gradually add complexity
- Never assume prior knowledge
- Use analogies and metaphors
- Repeat key points in different ways
